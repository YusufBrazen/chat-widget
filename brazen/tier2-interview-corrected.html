The Position

As Brazen grows, we are expanding our support team. This role will be our first fully dedicated Tier 2 Support Engineer and will have a tremendous impact on our customer experience and processes. In this role, you will handle complex escalated support issues to resolution in support of our customers. 

Responsibilities

  • Relationships: Build strong relationships between internal teams.
  • Issue Resolution 
    • Handle tickets with disciplined, process driven methodologies escalated from Tier 1 and other channels to completion (regardless of the fix): review escalation, investigate and resolve utilizing historical database records and SOPs, document interactions in Jira and ZenDesk, provide timely resolution to customer, and ensure customer satisfaction
  • Improve the Product
    • Capture customer insights/feedback and share with our product team
    • Submit bug reports and alert us to patterns of problems
  • Continual learning and improvement
    • Continuously update knowledge base to improve processes and ensure known fixes are current.
    • Update bug and enhancements in Jira
  • Prioritization
    • Triage issues in regards to deadlines, severity, SLAs, and other context.

Qualifications

  • Help Desk: Minimum of 1-3 years in-the-field experience experience supporting browser-based software (SaaS) 
  • Issue Tracking: Minimum of 1-3 years experience working daily in ticketing system (like Jira or Zen Desk)
  • Customer Focus: 
    • 1+ years of customer service experience  and customer service passion
    • Excited to help Brazen users by resolving complex inquiries and highly sensitive escalations
    • Heart of a teacher: Patient, thoughtful, encouraging, and calm.
  • Communication: 
    • Amazing communicator on the phone, chat, Slack, and email
    • Good listener, quick and clear writer, able to dissect customer descriptions into root problems to begin troubleshooting
  • Computer / Technical Literary: Proficient in basic IT troubleshooting
  • Time Management: ability to manage time and deciding amongst competing priorities.
  • Autonomous, Analytical, and Organized:
    • Strong analytical, quick learner, problem solving, and organizational skills
    • Self-starter with the ability to drive resolution to existing and potentially never before encountered issues quickly.
    • Works well under pressure and in a high-paced environment

Preferred Qualifications

  • Tier 2 experience 
  • Experience troubleshooting SSO and integrations
  • Customer-facing support for an external Business-to-business (B2B) context
  • SQL experience
